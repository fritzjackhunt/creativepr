Core Features

Guaranteed & Secure Payments

All funds are held in Thecreativepr until influencers complete your campaign. We make sure influencers adhere to policy and that you are satisfied with their deliverables.

Transparent Pricing.

Influencer pricing is available on Thecreativepr upfront. When you see what influencers charge other brands, you don't have to be taken advantage of.

Thecreativepr Score.

We continuously check influencers for the authenticity of their audience. Our proprietary Thecreativepr Score ranks influencers based on past campaign success and results.

Scalable Campaigns.

We offer the ability to hire multiple influencers at once. Collab with as low as few dollars, and upwards of $10k at a time. 5 to 5000 influencers at once!

5 Social Media Channels.

We currently work with all major social media channels including Youtube, TikTok, Facebook, and Twitter.

Tracking & Metrics.

Post tracking and statistics are available on campaign and individual collab levels. Know exactly which influencer brings the most ROI, and don't waste your budget.

Audience Demographics.

Filter audience by language, country, age, sex, and gender allowing you to choose influencers with audience that match your target market.

Direct Contact.

Influencers are directly signed up on Thecreativepr. You don't need to deal with unresponsive influencers and chase DMs all day long, just set up your campaigns and schedule it for publishing!

Friendly Customer Support.

Our support got your back! It can be tough to deal with influencers and hold them accountable, make sure they post on time, following brief guidelines, etc. we at Thecreativepr do that for you.

A Campaign Application is a proposal that a creator sends to you during the application process. It includes their portfolio link, short message, and other info. You can view all of your campaign applications in your Thecreativepr Messenger.

Thecreativepr Marketplace is a store like platform where you can pick existing creators gigs to work with. You can browse through the marketplace to find creators that match your goals.
